WELCOME

Hi! My name is Elvira Vriesema, nice to meet you.
I am studying Communication and Multimedia Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences. In this portfolio you'll find my process and finished products for the course: HCI1 technologies.

1. Human Computer Interaction (HCI) researches the design and use of computer technology, focused on the interfaces between people (users) and computers.

Small introduction

In the first five weeks we receive lectures of five different topics. Research and a homework assignment will be done of each topic. All elaborations of this can be found under 'topics'.

In this course I will also participate in 4 workshops that cover the practical and technical tools and techniques needed for the final product, see 'workshops' in the menu. We also did an excursion which can be found at 'excursion'.

Lab weeks were scheduled in the last two weeks. We worked continuously on our interactive concept in a group of five (international) students on a location outside school. The weeks ended with an exhibition of the final interactive installation. The process, product and my reflection can be read under 'lab weeks'.

Fact: The groupmembers were from the Netherlands, USA and Germany. That explains my 'welcome' text.

Expectations & Goals

We will learn how to use Human Computer Interaction Technologies and explore the exciting new possibilities of interactive technologies. The focus is on technology, so we will discover opportunities for new types of interaction. I expect the two working weeks will be very intense and maybe my brain will explode afterwards, but I think it will be worth it.


I hope to let my creative mind work, combined with the inspiration that technology offers within this project to come up with an interactive concept. I will create a detailed concept for a valuable, innovative interaction.
